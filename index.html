Who We Are

An indigenous Non-Governmental Organization, founded in 2009 as a social development focused Organization by a group of 7 Community Development Volunteers aimed at addressing extreme poverty, illiteracy & diseases especially among the HIV/AIDS vulnerable children, unemployed youth, women and adults in rural communities of East Central (EC) region of Uganda.


Education

Education is vital for future success and well being. It empowers children, drives social change and breaks the cycle of poverty.

HIV/AIDS & Health

This is a great concern as the country makes strides towards elimination of HIV as a public threat by 2030 to have a healthy living.

Child Rights

Children are neither a possesion to parents nor of the state,nor are they mere people in making, they have equal status as members of the society.

Socio-Economic Empowerment

Skills development is key to improving productivity, employability and income earning opportunities thus enhancing food security.
